== Description ==

WP MobileMe Gallery is the so long waited plugin to include albums and photos hosted on Apple MobileMe service. Easy to use, it is fully customable.

It is finally possible to display your photo and movie galleries directly on your website and to enable your visitors to navigate through your albums and photos without leaving your website. You would prefer to display just an album or even just a photo? No problem. This plugin will do it.

The insertion is done using shortcodes which enables to personalize the display. But sincerly, who likes to type shortcodes? The plugin propose a tool in the text editor which enables to insert easily the shortcodes. Isn't it fantastic?

Even simplier, you just want to display the lastest photos or random photos from your gallery in a widget? Guess what? That's possible!

More Info : http://www.sebastiengillard.fr/webdesigner/wp-mobileme-plugin/

== Installation ==

1. Upload `wp-mobileme-gallery` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. Enter your MobileMe login in the 'WP MobileMe Gallery' Option Page.
1. Add the 'wp-mobileme-gallery' widget to your website or insert a gallery, album or photo in an article or a page.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Will I need to enter my MobileMe password? =

No. Only your login is required to get the public photos.
